Our Mountain Retreat Campus

Where learning meets spiritual formation in nature's classroom

A Unique Learning Environment

Experience theological education in a transformative mountain retreat setting designed to deepen your faith and sharpen your calling.

On The Mountain Revival Centre is situated at a peaceful mountain camping facility that provides the perfect environment for intensive study, spiritual reflection, and community building. Away from the distractions of everyday life, students can fully immerse themselves in their theological education while experiencing the beauty and tranquility of God's creation.

Our campus combines the simplicity of outdoor camping with modern educational facilities, creating a unique space where students can encounter God, engage with scripture, and be equipped for ministry in an atmosphere of peace and focus.

Campus Features

Everything you need for transformative learning and spiritual growth

Accommodation Options

Comfortable camping accommodations, rustic cabins, dormitory-style lodging, and limited private rooms to suit different preferences and needs.

Accommodation Options

Comfortable lodging designed to enhance your learning experience

Camping

For those who enjoy the outdoor experience, bring your own tent and camp under the stars. Ablution facilities with hot showers are conveniently located throughout the camping areas.

Rustic Cabins

Simple but comfortable cabins sleeping 4-6 people, providing shelter while maintaining the camping atmosphere. Equipped with beds, basic furniture, and heating for colder months.

Dormitory Style

Shared dormitory accommodations with bunk beds, lockers, and communal bathroom facilities. Perfect for those seeking community and affordability.

Private Rooms

Limited availability private rooms for couples, families, or those requiring special accommodations. These include en-suite bathrooms and basic amenities.

Note: All accommodation options provide bedding, but students may bring their own sleeping bags for camping. Towels and personal toiletries should be brought by students.

Contact Sessions Schedule

How the residential learning experience works

Residential Intensives

Students attend 3-5 day residential intensive sessions on campus throughout the year. These sessions combine lectures, workshops, practical ministry training, and community worship.

Distance Learning

Between residential sessions, students engage in guided distance learning with reading assignments, online discussions, and research projects, supported by faculty mentorship.

Practical Ministry

Students apply their learning in their local church context between sessions, receiving ongoing mentorship and supervision from assigned faculty members.

Typical Contact Session Schedule

  • Day 1: Arrival, orientation, and evening worship
  • Days 2-4: Morning devotions, lectures, workshops, practical training, and evening activities
  • Day 5: Final sessions, assessments, communion service, and departure

What to Bring

  • Personal toiletries
  • Towels and washcloth

Please Leave at Home

To maintain a focused learning environment, we ask that you minimize distractions during contact sessions. While phones and electronics are permitted, we encourage limited use to maximize your spiritual and academic experience.
