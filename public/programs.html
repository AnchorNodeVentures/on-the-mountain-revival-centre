Certificate in Ministry

Foundation training for those beginning their ministry journey

Duration: 1 year
Credits: 30
Level: NQF 5
Mode: Contact Sessions

Program Overview

The Certificate in Ministry provides foundational training for those called to serve in Christian ministry. This program introduces students to essential biblical knowledge, theological concepts, and practical ministry skills. Perfect for emerging leaders, lay ministers, and those seeking to deepen their understanding of scripture and ministry practice.

What You'll Learn

  • ✓ Biblical foundations and scriptural interpretation
  • ✓ Core theological concepts and Christian doctrine
  • ✓ Introduction to pastoral ministry and leadership
  • ✓ Practical ministry skills and spiritual formation

Who Should Apply

This program is ideal for:

  • Those beginning their ministry journey
  • Church volunteers and lay leaders
  • Youth workers and children's ministry workers
  • Anyone seeking formal biblical training

Associate Degree in Ministry

Comprehensive ministry training for effective leadership

Duration: 3 years
Credits: 28
Level: NQF 5
Mode: Blended + Residential

Program Overview

The Associate Degree in Ministry offers the most comprehensive training program at On The Mountain Revival Centre. This program combines rigorous theological education with extensive practical ministry experience, preparing graduates for senior leadership positions in churches, missions, and Christian organizations. Students receive personalized mentorship and benefit from intensive residential sessions at our mountain retreat campus.

What You'll Learn

  • ✓ Comprehensive biblical and theological studies
  • ✓ Advanced ministry leadership and administration
  • ✓ Cross-cultural ministry and missions
  • ✓ Contemporary issues in ministry and theology
  • ✓ Practical ministry through supervised field work

Program Features

  • Personalized mentorship throughout the program
  • Residential intensives at our mountain retreat campus
  • Supervised practical ministry experience
  • Networking with ministry leaders and practitioners
  • Access to comprehensive theological library resources

Degree

Degree in Divinity

Advanced qualification for senior ministry leadership

Duration: 4 years total
Credits: 20
Level: Advanced
Mode: Research + Residential

Program Overview

The Degree in Divinity represents the highest level of theological education offered at On The Mountain Revival Centre. This program is designed for experienced ministry leaders seeking advanced theological knowledge and research capabilities. Students engage in independent research, advanced biblical and theological studies, and produce original scholarship that contributes to the broader Christian community.

What You'll Learn

  • ✓ Advanced biblical languages and textual criticism
  • ✓ Contemporary theological thought and philosophy
  • ✓ Research methodologies in theology and ministry
  • ✓ Senior-level ministry leadership and strategy
  • ✓ Dissertation research and scholarly writing

Admission Requirements

  • Completion of Associate Degree or equivalent qualification
  • Minimum 5 years of active ministry experience
  • Demonstrated leadership in church or ministry context
  • Research proposal and interview
Apply for Divinity Degree
