About Us

Equipping and empowering leaders through transformative training

Our Vision

To equip and empower leaders through transformative training in a unique mountain retreat setting.

At On The Mountain Revival Centre, we believe that effective ministry requires more than just academic knowledge—it demands spiritual depth, practical wisdom, and a transformative encounter with God. Our unique approach combines rigorous theological education with immersive spiritual formation in a mountain camping environment that removes distractions and creates space for deep learning and personal growth.

We envision a new generation of ministry leaders who are both theologically sound and spiritually vibrant, equipped to bring revival and transformation to their communities.

Our Mission

To provide SAQA accredited theological training that combines academic excellence with practical ministry preparation in a transformative retreat environment.

Our mission is to develop well-rounded ministry leaders through:

  • ✓ Quality theological education that is both academically rigorous and spiritually enriching
  • ✓ Practical ministry training through hands-on experience and mentorship
  • ✓ Spiritual formation in a peaceful mountain retreat setting
  • ✓ Community building through residential contact sessions
  • ✓ Ongoing mentorship and pastoral support

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in theological education, ensuring our programs meet SAQA accreditation requirements and exceed expectations.

Transformation

We believe education should transform lives, not just inform minds. Our approach integrates spiritual formation with academic learning.

Community

What Makes Us Different

A unique approach to theological education

Mountain Retreat Setting

Unlike traditional Bible colleges, our campus is located at a peaceful mountain camping facility. This unique environment creates space for deep spiritual encounters, focused learning, and life-changing transformation away from daily distractions.

Flexible Study Modes

We understand that many called to ministry are already serving in churches. Our contact session model allows you to continue your current ministry while pursuing formal theological education through periodic residential intensives.

Personalized Mentorship

Every student is paired with an experienced ministry mentor who provides guidance, accountability, and support throughout their journey. This one-on-one relationship ensures personalized growth and development.

Community-Centered Learning

Our residential contact sessions create a tight-knit learning community. Students form deep relationships, learn from each other's experiences, and build networks that last throughout their ministry careers.
